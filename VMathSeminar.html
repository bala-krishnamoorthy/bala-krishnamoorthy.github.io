WSU Vancouver Mathematics and Statistics Seminar (Fall 2025)

Welcome to the WSU Vancouver Seminar in Mathematics and Statistics! The Seminar meets on Wednesdays at 2:10–3:00 PM in VUB 124 (unless mentioned otherwise). This is the Undergraduate Building (marked "N" in the campus map). The seminar is open to the public, and here is some information for visitors.

Students could sign up for Math 592 (titled Seminar in Analysis) for 1 credit. Talks will be given by external speakers, as well as by WSUV faculty and students. Contact the organizer Bala Krishnamoorthy if you want to invite a speaker, or to give a talk.
