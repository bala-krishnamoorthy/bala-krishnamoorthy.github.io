Math 273: Calculus of Function of Several Variables

Course Description

This is Calculus III, and continues where Calculus II (Math 172) ended. We will cover most sections from Chapters 13, 14, and 15 (from the text). Topics include partial derivatives, multiple integrals, and integration in vector fields. Applications relevant to science and engineering will be emphasized. Some exposure will also be given to computer tools for exploring the concepts.
